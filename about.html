About Us

Brijesh Banger Memorial Hospital is one of the largest multi-super specialty hospitals in Bhilwara. It is a tertiary care center dedicated to the memory of Shri Brijesh Ji Banger, offering comprehensive services in Trauma, Orthopedics, Neurosurgery, Plastic Surgery, Cardiology, Obstetrics & Gynecology, GI Surgery, Medicine,Skin and VD, Pediatrics, Opthalmology ,Urology,ENT and Critical Care.
