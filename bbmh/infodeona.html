Dr. Deona Jajoo

Specialty: Consultant Obstetrician And Gynaecologist

​Dr. Deona Jajoo is a Consultant Obstetrician and Gynecologist at Brijesh Banger Memorial Hospital (BBMH) in Bhilwara, Rajasthan. She holds an MBBS degree and has completed a Fellowship in Minimal Access Surgery (FMAS), specializing as a Laparoscopic Surgeon. ​ With over 11 years of experience, Dr. Jajoo offers a range of gynecological services, including pregnancy and childbirth care, as well as laparoscopic procedures.
