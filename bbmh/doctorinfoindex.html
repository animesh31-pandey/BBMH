Dr. Alok Mittal

Specialty: Sr. Consultant Physician

Dr. Alok Mittal is the Senior Consultant Physician and Medical Superintendent at BBMH, Bhilwara. With an MBBS from SNMC, Jodhpur, and MD from SMS Medical College, Jaipur, he specializes in diabetes, hypertension, and obesity management, with over 24 years of experience.

Education: MBBS in 1988 from SNMC, Jodhpur, and earned his MD in Medicine in 2000 from SMS Medical College, Jaipur.
